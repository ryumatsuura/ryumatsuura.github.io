### [Masala Merge](https://github.com/devdatalab/masala-merge){:target="_blank"}

I developed a primitive version of an R package for masala-merge, a fuzzy string matching algorithm designed for Indian place and person names.

### [Japanese Pension Simulator](https://ryumatsuura.github.io/code/pension-simulator.html){:target="_blank"}

I developed a Python-based simulator to compare the Japanese pension system with the opportunity cost of investment.

### [Japanese Medical Cost Simulator](https://ryumatsuura.github.io/code/medical-cost-simulator.html){:target="_blank"}

I developed a browser-based simulator for the proposed high-cost medical expense reform, using annual income and monthly medical spending to estimate monthly and annual out-of-pocket costs.
